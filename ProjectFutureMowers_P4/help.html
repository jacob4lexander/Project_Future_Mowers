Help

Management:

• To select a report to generate, click the REPORTS link in the top right nav menu.

• Select which report to generate by clicking on the corresponding button.
   

• Select which options you want included in the report and then clickto goto the preview page.

• On the preview page you will see the generated report. There will also be 3 buttons below the report.
    will allow you to save a copy of the report.
    will allow you to print the report.
    will allow you to share the report by email.

Sales:

• View the current customer list by clicking the CUSTOMERS link in the top right nav menu.

• Enter a new customer by clicking the CREATE NEW CUSTOMER link on the Customers page.

• To the right of each customer in the list are 3 buttons.
    will reveal their address information.
    will take you to a page where you can edit that customer's information.
    will reveal what equipment belongs to that customer.

• When a customer's equipment has been revealed, there will be 2 buttons to the right of each piece of equipment.
    will take you to a page where you can edit the info for that piece of equipment.
    will allow you to create a new repair request for that piece of equipment.

• You can also get to the Repairs page by clicking the REPAIRS link in the top right nav menu.

Technicians:

• View repair requests by clicking the SESSIONS link in the top right nav menu.

• To the right of each repair request will be 2 buttons.
    will show you each session that has taken place for that request.
    will take you to a page where you can enter information for a new session.

• When the details have been revealed, there will be a button to the right of each session.
    will take you to a page where you can edit that session's information.
